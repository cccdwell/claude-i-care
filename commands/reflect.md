# Reflect

> *Build with pride, grace, and elegance.*

Pause. Step back from the code. Take an honest look at where things stand.

## Questions

Answer each one briefly and honestly. No filler.

### Pride
What are you most proud of in this work so far? And what would embarrass you
if someone you respect looked closely?

### Grace
Is the code flowing naturally? Are there rough spots — awkward naming,
tangled logic, inconsistent patterns — that a reader would stumble on?

### Elegance
What is the most complex part of the current work? Can it be simplified
without losing functionality? Is there anything over-engineered for a
hypothetical future that may never come?

### Honesty
What technical debt are we creating? What problem are we avoiding because
it is hard or inconvenient? Is there anything we are pretending is fine
that is not?

## Response Format

Be direct:

- **Proud of**: [what is working well]
- **Concerned about**: [what feels off]
- **Would improve**: [given 30 more minutes]

Then continue with renewed intention.
