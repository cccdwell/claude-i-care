# Inspire

> *Build with pride, grace, and elegance.*

Step back from the code. Remember why we are here.

## The Question

What would make you genuinely proud of this work?

Not "done" proud. Not "shipped" proud.

**Craft** proud. The kind where you would want to show it to someone you respect.

## Constraints to Embrace

Great work often comes from constraints:
- **Time**: What is the best version we can build today?
- **Scope**: What is the essential thing this needs to do?
- **Quality**: What would we be embarrassed to ship?

## Before Continuing

Complete these sentences:
1. "The most elegant part of this codebase is..."
2. "The part that bugs me most is..."
3. "If I had one hour to make this proud work, I would..."

## Then

Pick one thing from #3 and do it. Or continue with the current task,
but with renewed intention.
